Directions

The wedding venue is located in San Luis Obispo. A common nickname
for San Luis Obispo is SLO. You can arrive at SLO by plane or car.
Please see more detailed directions below.





Plane

Car

The local airport in SLO is San Luis Obispo
County Regional Airport. The airport code
for airline searches (Kayak, Expedia,
Travelocity, etc.) is SBP. The commercial
regional carriers for this airport are US
Airways and United. The airport is 13.9 miles
away from Santa Margarita Ranch (wedding
venue). This is approximately a 20 minute
drive. Once you arrive, take the CA-227 N/Broad
St and Osos St. to US-101 N. Follow the US
101-N to CA-58 E. Take the CA-58 exit from
US-101 N. Continue on CA-58E. Drive to Yerba
Buena Ave. and turn left. You will enter the
ranch and follow the pathway until you reach
the parking area near the barn.

FROM LOS ANGELES: 199 mi/Approx 3 hrs
Take US-101 N until merging onto CA-58 E
toward Santa Margarita Ranch. Drive to Yerba
Buena Avenue and turn left onto the ranch.
Drive down the long path until you arrive at
the venues’ parking lot.

FROM SAN FRANCISO: 224 mi/Approx 3.5 hrs
Take US-101 S towards San Jose. Take the
CA-58 exit toward Santa Margarita. Merge
onto El Camino Real. Turn left onto Yerba
Buena. Drive down the long path until you
arrive at the venues’ parking lot.
